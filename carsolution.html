Lamborghini Aventador

The exterior of the Lamborghini Aventador SVJ Roadster Xago Edition took 120 hours to produce and features a specially-created fading hexagonal silver effect, with a unique Ad Personam contrasting color livery for each car and gloss black Nireo Ad Personam rims. The interior, created in 80 hours, features an exclusive seat pattern with the hexagonita pattern, and special contrasting color to match the exterior. Each of the ten Aventador SVJ Roadster Xago models will be identified by a numbered plate.
